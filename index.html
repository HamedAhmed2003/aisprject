Tesla Model S

The Tesla Model S is an all-electric luxury sedan known for its incredible acceleration, autopilot features, and zero emissions. With a range of up to 405 miles and a minimalist design, it's a pioneer in electric vehicles.

Ferrari 488 Spider

The Ferrari 488 Spider is a mid-engine sports car with a retractable hardtop. Powered by a 3.9-liter twin-turbo V8 engine producing 661 horsepower, it offers exhilarating performance and Italian craftsmanship.

Lamborghini Huracan

The Lamborghini Huracan is a supercar with aggressive styling and a 5.2-liter V10 engine delivering 602 horsepower. It's designed for track days and street driving, with advanced aerodynamics and a thrilling exhaust note.

Porsche 911

The Porsche 911 is an iconic sports car with rear-engine layout and a history spanning decades. Available in various trims, it offers precise handling, luxury interiors, and engines from 379 to over 600 horsepower.

Bugatti Chiron

The Bugatti Chiron is a hypercar with a quad-turbo 8.0-liter W16 engine producing 1,479 horsepower. It can reach speeds over 300 mph and features bespoke luxury with carbon fiber construction.

Koenigsegg Agera RS

The Koenigsegg Agera RS is a Swedish hypercar with a 5.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine outputting 1,341 horsepower. It's one of the fastest production cars, with a top speed of 278 mph and advanced aerodynamics.

McLaren 720S

The McLaren 720S is a supercar with a 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine delivering 710 horsepower. It features carbon fiber monocoque, active aerodynamics, and a cockpit designed for maximum driver engagement.

Aston Martin DB11

The Aston Martin DB11 is a grand tourer with a 5.2-liter twin-turbo V12 engine producing 608 horsepower. It combines British luxury, sharp handling, and a stunning design inspired by the DB9.

Rolls-Royce Ghost

The Rolls-Royce Ghost is the pinnacle of luxury sedans, featuring a 6.75-liter twin-turbo V12 engine with 563 horsepower. It offers silent operation, bespoke interiors, and unparalleled comfort.

Bentley Continental GT

The Bentley Continental GT is a luxury coupe with a 6.0-liter twin-turbo W12 engine delivering 626 horsepower. It provides a blend of performance, elegance, and handcrafted interiors.

Mercedes-Benz S-Class

The Mercedes-Benz S-Class is a flagship luxury sedan with advanced technology, including autonomous driving features and a smooth 3.0-liter V6 engine. It's renowned for comfort, safety, and innovation.

BMW 7 Series

The BMW 7 Series is a luxury sedan offering a range of engines from efficient diesels to powerful V8s. It features cutting-edge tech, adaptive suspension, and a driver-focused cockpit.

Audi A8

The Audi A8 is a luxury sedan with quattro all-wheel drive and engines up to 3.0-liter V6 turbo. It includes matrix LED headlights, virtual cockpit, and semi-autonomous driving capabilities.

Jaguar XJ

The Jaguar XJ is a luxury saloon with a 3.0-liter V6 supercharged engine producing 340 horsepower. It offers British elegance, advanced infotainment, and a smooth ride.

Lexus LS

The Lexus LS is a luxury sedan known for its hybrid powertrains and exceptional comfort. The LS 500h combines a 3.5-liter V6 with electric motors for a refined and efficient driving experience.

Cadillac Escalade

The Cadillac Escalade is a full-size luxury SUV with a 6.2-liter V8 engine delivering 420 horsepower. It features advanced tech, spacious interiors, and towing capabilities.

Chevrolet Corvette

The Chevrolet Corvette is an American sports car with a mid-engine layout and a 6.2-liter V8 engine producing 495 horsepower. It's lightweight, agile, and offers thrilling performance.

Ford Mustang

The Ford Mustang is an iconic muscle car with a 5.0-liter V8 engine delivering 460 horsepower. It represents American heritage with modern tech, including SYNC infotainment.

Dodge Challenger

The Dodge Challenger is a muscle car with a 6.4-liter Hemi V8 engine producing 485 horsepower. It features retro styling, powerful performance, and a range of trims for different tastes.

Toyota Supra

The Toyota Supra is a sports car with a 3.0-liter twin-turbo inline-6 engine delivering 335 horsepower. It's known for reliability, handling, and a rich motorsport history.
